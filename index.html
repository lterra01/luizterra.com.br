About

Built from telecom sales, international markets and company creation.

Luiz Fernando Coelho Terra built his career from an early sales role at CelPlan Technologies into regional leadership across Brazil and Latin America, working with telecom operators, outsourcing groups and financial-sector accounts.

His international track record includes 3+ years based in Mexico City, public presentations at contact center congresses in Colombia and Mexico, the USD 2M Telesur/Suriname prepaid mobile and customer-care project, and the creation of Anew Brasil from zero into a multi-client operation.

Today he leads international sales at Khomp, with focus on SBC, AI-powered AMD, Genesys AppFoundry integration and the US market. In parallel, he studies Artificial Intelligence at Cruzeiro do Sul EAD, with an active thesis on forest monitoring using satellite imagery for Parque do Ibirapuera.
